1. Fix Sponsor Card Behavior (Always Show All Sponsor Actions)
Problem

The current sponsor card behavior remembers the last action and replaces the buttons with a state like:

Onsite Meeting Opening...
Reopen

This hides other available options such as:

Online Meeting

Request Information

This behavior does not provide value and reduces usability.

Attendees should always see all available actions for that sponsor.

Required Behavior

Sponsor cards should always display all available actions based on sponsor configuration.

The UI must never replace buttons with "Reopen" or similar states.

Instead, the interface should remain stable.

Example:

Schedule Onsite Meeting
Online Meeting
Request Information

These buttons should always remain visible.

If a User Already Took an Action

Instead of replacing the button, show a status indicator next to the button.

Examples:

After requesting onsite meeting
Schedule Onsite Meeting  ✓ Requested
Online Meeting
Request Information
After requesting information
Schedule Onsite Meeting
Online Meeting
Request Information ✓ Sent
After scheduling a meeting
Schedule Onsite Meeting ✓ Scheduled
Online Meeting
Request Information
Do NOT Use "Reopen"

Remove the entire "Reopen" behavior.

Users can simply click the button again if they want to schedule another meeting.

Source of Truth

The buttons must continue to respect sponsor configuration:

Sponsor settings determine if the card shows:

Onsite Meeting
Online Meeting
Request Information

Example sponsor configurations:

Sponsor	Buttons
Platinum	Onsite + Online + Info
Bronze	Onsite + Info
Digital	Online only
Result

Sponsor cards should be stable.

They should never hide other actions.
