Replit Instructions
Update Concierge Welcome Page Layout (Next Step Section + Topic Selection)

Improve the Welcome / Registration Confirmation page so the Next Step section is more prominent and easier to understand.

The goal is to make the attendee immediately recognize that selecting topics is the next action and visually connect those topics to the sponsors shown below.

1. Make the "Next Step" Section More Prominent

The Next Step section currently blends into the page and should be visually emphasized.

Update styling

Increase the prominence of the section header.

Current
NEXT STEP
Make the most of your time at the conference.
Required

Use stronger styling for Next Step.

Example improvements:

increase font size

use stronger weight

maintain uppercase styling

optionally add a small icon if consistent with design language

Example:

NEXT STEP
Choose the topics you're interested in.

or

NEXT STEP
Tell us what you're interested in.

This should feel like a clear instruction.

2. Delete the Instruction Sentence

Remove the following sentence completely:

Select the topics you're interested in and we'll highlight participating sponsors aligned with those areas.

This sentence is redundant and makes the page feel cluttered.

The interface itself will communicate the purpose.

3. Combine the Heading Text

Currently the page displays:

Make the most of your time at the conference.

and later:

What are you interested in?

These should be combined into a single clearer instruction.

Replace both lines with:
Make the most of your time at the conference.
What topics are you interested in?

Or preferably a single stronger line:

Make the most of your time at the conference. What topics interest you?

This reduces visual fragmentation.

4. Move the Topic Selection Into the Lightly Shaded Section

The topic selection buttons currently appear below the header but should instead appear inside the lightly shaded "Next Step" section.

Required layout

The shaded section should contain:

NEXT STEP

Make the most of your time at the conference. What topics interest you?

[ AI ] [ Compliance ] [ Customer Service ] [ Legal ] [ Regulatory ]

This visually groups the instruction and the action together.

5. Ensure Topic Buttons Are Clearly Visible

The topic buttons should remain styled as clickable filter chips.

Requirements:

keep current rounded chip style

maintain spacing between buttons

allow wrapping to multiple rows if needed

maintain hover and selected states

6. Improve Visual Hierarchy

Within the shaded section the layout should be:

Row 1
NEXT STEP
Row 2
Make the most of your time at the conference. What topics interest you?
Row 3

Topic buttons

[ AI ] [ Compliance ] [ Customer Service ] [ Legal ] [ Regulatory ]
7. Maintain Section Background Styling

Keep the lightly shaded background used for the "Next Step" area.

This background visually separates the action step from:

the registration confirmation header

the sponsor section below

8. Keep Sponsor Section Unchanged

Do not modify the Meet With Our Sponsors section below.

It should continue to display:

sponsor cards

sponsor levels

meeting buttons

request information options

The only change is that topic selection now sits clearly above it.

10. Acceptance Criteria

This update is complete when:

the "Next Step" section is visually stronger

the instruction sentence has been removed

the topic selection appears inside the shaded section

the two headings are combined into one clearer instruction

the sponsor cards remain unchanged

the page feels simpler and easier to understand

UX Goal

After this change, attendees should immediately understand:

They are registered

Their next action is selecting topics
