Sponsorship Deliverables Tab — Corrections and Improvements

The Deliverables tab in the Sponsor Dashboard needs several structural, UX, and data-ownership corrections.

Currently the page has problems such as:

sections collapsing/expanding

incorrect "Awaiting Your Input" statuses

missing configuration fields

legacy notices that should be removed

quantities placed inconsistently

missing admin entry fields for Converge deliverables

The goal is to make this tab function as a clear operational checklist for sponsors and Converge Events.

1. Expand All Sections by Default

Currently sections such as:

Company Profile

Event Participation

Speaking & Content

Meetings & Introductions

Marketing & Branding

Post-Event Deliverables

Compliance

require clicking to expand.

This creates unnecessary friction.

Required change

All sections should be expanded by default and not collapsible.

The page should behave like a deliverables checklist rather than an accordion.

2. Move Quantity Display

Currently quantities appear bottom right of sections (example: “4 items”). 

Sample SPonsorship Deliverables

This is not helpful.

Required change

Move quantity information into the section header.

Example:

Company Profile (4 Deliverables)
Event Participation (2 Deliverables)
Marketing & Branding (3 Deliverables)

Remove the small item count on the right.

3. Correct Deliverable Ownership Logic

Several items currently show “Awaiting Your Input” even though they are Converge Events responsibilities.

These statuses must be corrected.
