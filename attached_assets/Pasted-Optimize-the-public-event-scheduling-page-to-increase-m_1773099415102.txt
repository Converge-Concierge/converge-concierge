Optimize the public event scheduling page to increase meeting conversion.

Important rules:
- Do not redesign the app
- Do not break sponsor filtering, sponsor cards, sponsor profile links, or scheduling flows
- Preserve all current working functionality
- Do not change sponsor tier naming/badge language in this task
- Exclude any sponsor-badge copy changes from this update

Goal:
Improve meeting conversion on the event page with stronger but sponsor-neutral messaging, clearer section hierarchy, and easier browsing.

==================================================
1. ADD SOFT SCARCITY LANGUAGE
==================================================

Below the main concierge message, add this line:

"Sponsors are offering a limited number of private meeting slots during the event."

Requirements:
- keep the tone soft and truthful
- do not imply fake scarcity
- do not imply some sponsors are more important than others
- place this directly below the existing intro copy

Approved top copy block should read:

Make the most of your time at the event by scheduling meetings with participating sponsors.

Select the topics you're interested in and we'll highlight participating sponsors aligned with those areas.

Sponsors are offering a limited number of private meeting slots during the event.

==================================================
2. ADD A DYNAMIC SUBHEADING ABOVE SPONSOR CARDS
==================================================

Above the sponsor cards, add a sponsor-results label.

Default state:
"Sponsors Available for Meetings"

When one or more topic chips are selected:
"Available Sponsors Matching Your Interests"

Requirements:
- this should sit directly above the sponsor card grid
- keep styling simple and consistent with the page
- do not use wording like "Top Sponsors" or "Recommended Sponsors"

==================================================
3. ADD A MICRO-TRUST STATEMENT
==================================================

Directly below the topic chips, add:

"All meetings are private 30-minute conversations with sponsor representatives during the event."

Purpose:
- reduce hesitation
- clarify what the meeting actually is
- reassure attendees before they click

Keep this line small, clean, and easy to scan.

==================================================
4. ADD A “SHOW ALL SPONSORS” OPTION
==================================================

Below or beside the topic chips, add a small text link or subtle button:

"Show All Sponsors"

Behavior:
- clears all selected topics
- restores the full sponsor list for that event
- does not break existing chip filtering logic

This is for attendees who prefer browsing rather than filtering first.

==================================================
5. TIGHTEN SECTION SPACING
==================================================

Improve visual scanning by adding a little more spacing between:

- the main concierge message block
- the "What are you interested in?" heading
- the sponsor results label
- the sponsor card grid

Suggested adjustment:
- add approximately 12px–16px of additional vertical spacing where needed
- do not make the page taller than necessary
- maintain above-the-fold friendliness where possible

==================================================
6. PRESERVE SPONSOR-NEUTRAL LANGUAGE
==================================================

Do NOT add wording like:
- Top sponsors
- Best sponsors
- Recommended sponsors
- Worth meeting
- Ranking numbers

Use only neutral language such as:
- participating sponsors
- sponsors available for meetings
- sponsors matching your interests

==================================================
7. KEEP CURRENT SPONSOR SORTING
==================================================

Do not change the current sponsor sorting logic in this task.

If multiple sponsors match the selected topics, keep the existing tier-priority ordering:
- Platinum
- Gold
- Silver
- Bronze

This remains in place, but do not call attention to it in the UI.

==================================================
8. FINAL TARGET LAYOUT
==================================================

The page should read in this order:

EVENT HEADER

Make the most of your time at the event by scheduling meetings with participating sponsors.

Select the topics you're interested in and we'll highlight participating sponsors aligned with those areas.

Sponsors are offering a limited number of private meeting slots during the event.

What are you interested in?

[ topic chips ]
[ Show All Sponsors ]

All meetings are private 30-minute conversations with sponsor representatives during the event.

Sponsors Available for Meetings
or
Available Sponsors Matching Your Interests

[ sponsor cards ]

==================================================
9. SUCCESS CRITERIA
==================================================

After implementation:
- soft scarcity language is added
- sponsor results label appears above the cards
- trust statement appears below the chips
- Show All Sponsors clears filters
- page spacing feels cleaner and easier to scan
- no sponsor-judgment language is introduced
- sponsor filtering and scheduling continue to work normally
